About Us

Since 2008, we have enjoyed serving our community of Windsor by providing everyday items at the lowest possible price, diverting unwanted goods from area landfills and sponsoring and partnering with local organizations that assist local citizens. Together, we have been making a difference and continue to find ways to help and support persons in need within our local community.
